About the company

The real estate agency provides services for the purchase, sale, and rental of real estate. The main clients are individuals, but it is also planned to provide services to legal entities. There is no specific seasonality in this business. The key is to build your work correctly and be flexible to external conditions and their changes.

When conducting a transaction, the agency helps its client to collect and timely process a package of documents, and to agree on the terms of the contract. It is the specialists who ensure that the documentation is properly completed in accordance with the current local and federal laws.

Realtors must keep the transaction confidential and must not disclose any confidential information they receive about a client or a transaction. It is also important to note that the agency is responsible for the safety of the documents that have been entrusted to them. Real estate professionals must research the market, search for offers, and monitor the demand for real estate.

Our-flat

For sale is a beautiful and stylish one-bedroom apartment with a designer renovation in the Central District of Moscow on Verkhnyaya Krasnoselskaya Street. It is ready for occupancy. The apartment is located on the 17th floor of an 18-storey panel building (including the technical floor), which was built in 1995. The ceiling height is 2.65 m. The apartment's windows face the courtyard, providing a quiet and cozy environment. You can arrange a resident parking in the Krasnoselsky district of the Central Administrative District - there will be free parking up to Bolshaya Lubyanka and Myasnitskaya streets. The apartment has everything you need for a comfortable stay: comfortable furniture, modern appliances, and a combined bathroom.

The area has a well-developed infrastructure: schools, kindergartens, and various shops are within walking distance. The Krasnoselskaya metro station is a 8-minute walk away, making it easy and convenient to get around the city. The area is also home to the historic Sokolniki Park, a health trail, the Great Rose Garden, and an ice rink.
